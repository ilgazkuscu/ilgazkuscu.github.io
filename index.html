A/B Testing

A higher conversion rate is not enough. Teams need evidence that a change caused the lift and what it means commercially.

What I built

A statistically rigorous analysis of an ad-versus-PSA conversion experiment, including two-proportion z-tests, chi-squared tests, bootstrap confidence intervals, and corrected subgroup checks.

Tools

Python, SciPy, hypothesis testing, bootstrap confidence intervals

Result

Measured a statistically significant +0.77 percentage-point conversion lift across 588K sessions (p < 0.0001) and translated the lift into projected monthly revenue impact.
