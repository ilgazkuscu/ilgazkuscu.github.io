How I think

The model comes near the end.

01

Start with the decision

I ask what would actually change if the analysis works: investment choice, product rollout, risk alert, policy interpretation, or operational priority.

02

Interrogate the data

I look for leakage, timing problems, class imbalance, missingness, false certainty, and whether the dataset represents the real world or just collection bias.

03

Then choose the model

I like strong baselines, interpretable comparisons, and models that can be defended to technical and business audiences.
